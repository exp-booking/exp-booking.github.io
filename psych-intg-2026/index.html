About

About the meeting

The Intelligent Perception and Integration Conference is a focused academic meeting bringing together researchers interested in perception, cognition, object recognition, intelligent systems, and integrative representations.

The meeting is intended as a compact forum for discussion, exchange, and future collaboration among researchers working on related questions from behavioral, computational, cognitive, and neuroscientific perspectives.
